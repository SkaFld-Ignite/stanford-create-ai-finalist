# Theme Alignment Proposal: AI+Education Summit 2026

**Date:** February 4, 2026
**Purpose:** Optimize Track 3 copy to align with [AI+Education Summit 2026](https://acceleratelearning.stanford.edu/conference/aieducation-summit-2026/) themes while preserving the existing narrative core.

## Executive Summary
We are presenting the day before the summit. By subtly weaving in the summit's specific lexicon ("Catalyst vs. Crutch," "Social Learning," "Universal Learning"), we position AI Studio Teams not just as a project, but as a **practical implementation of the summit's theoretical goals**.

## 1. The "Catalyst vs. Crutch" Alignment
**Summit Theme:** Session "Creativity in the Age of AI: Catalyst, Crutch, or Both?"
**Our Core:** "Teach to Fish" / Metacognition.

### Recommendation
Explicitly use the words "Catalyst" and "Crutch" in our "Teach to Fish" explanation. This answers the summit's central question before they even ask it.

**Target File:** `docs/track-3-career/solution.md` & `docs/track-3-career/pitch-narrative.md`

| Current Concept | Proposed Refinement |
| :--- | :--- |
| "Don't give them the fish. Teach them to fish." | "**From Crutch to Catalyst.** Traditional AI tools can be a crutch (giving the fish). We turn AI into a catalyst for critical thinking (teaching to fish)." |
| "The AI is a coach, not a crutch." (Slide 5 narrative) | **Keep & Amplify.** This line is perfect. Ensure it is delivered with emphasis. |

## 2. "Social Learning" & "Human Connection"
**Summit Theme:** "The Social Learning Revolution" & "Deepening human connection."
**Our Core:** Near-Peer Mentorship & Team-based work.

### Recommendation
Reframe our mentorship and team structure as "Social Learning." Emphasize that we use AI to *deepen* human connection, not replace it.

**Target File:** `docs/track-3-career/solution.md` (Mentorship Section)

| Current Text | Proposed Refinement |
| :--- | :--- |
| "Near-Peer Mentorship: 12th Graders → 10th Graders..." | "**Social Learning Architecture:** We use AI to scaffold *human* connection. 12th graders mentor 10th graders..." |

**Target File:** `docs/track-3-career/index.md` (Executive Summary)

| Current Text | Proposed Refinement |
| :--- | :--- |
| "This program puts educators and learners at the heart of AI design..." | "This program puts educators and learners at the heart of AI design—**leveraging 'Social Learning' to ensure AI deepens human connection** rather than replacing it." |

## 3. "Universal Learning" vs. Access
**Summit Theme:** Moving from "Universal Access" to "Universal Learning" and "Designing *with* and *for* learners."
**Our Core:** Equity / "Portfolios are the great equalizer."

### Recommendation
Shift language in the Equity section from just "Access" to "Universal Design for Learning (UDL)" and "Universal Learning."

**Target File:** `docs/track-3-career/equity.md`

| Current Text | Proposed Refinement |
| :--- | :--- |
| "AI Studio Teams creates structured pathways that provide access for all..." | "AI Studio Teams moves beyond access to **Universal Learning**. We create structured pathways designed *with* and *for* learners of all backgrounds..." |
| "Equity Design Principles" | "**Universal Design Principles**" |

## 4. "AI Literacy" & "Epistemic Fluency"
**Summit Theme:** "AI Quests... toward AI Literacy."
**Our Core:** "AI Discernment" / "Hallucination Detection."

### Recommendation
Connect our "Discernment" pillar to the broader concept of "AI Literacy."

**Target File:** `docs/track-3-career/learning-science.md`

| Current Text | Proposed Refinement |
| :--- | :--- |
| "AI literacy requires critical evaluation skills" | "**True AI Literacy requires Epistemic Fluency.** It's not just using tools; it's evaluating the *nature of knowledge* they produce." |
