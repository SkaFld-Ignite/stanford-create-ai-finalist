# Implementation Timeline

## 12-Month Project Plan

The El Segundo AI Academy curriculum implementation follows a phased approach ensuring thorough development, pilot testing, and scaled deployment within the 12-month grant period.

## Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1-3)

### Month 1: Project Launch

| Week | Activities | Deliverables |
|------|------------|--------------|
| 1 | Project kickoff; team onboarding | Project charter signed |
| 2 | Stakeholder mapping; advisory committee formation | Advisory committee roster |
| 3 | Baseline data collection; pre-assessments | Baseline report |
| 4 | Technology infrastructure setup | Platform ready for development |

**Key Milestones:**
- Advisory committee established with family, student, and community representation
- Baseline data collected on current AI education participation by demographic
- Development infrastructure operational

### Month 2: Curriculum Framework

| Week | Activities | Deliverables |
|------|------------|--------------|

**Key Milestones:**
- Curriculum aligned to state and national standards
- Assessment approach validated by learning scientists

## Critical Path Items

| Item | Dependency | Risk Mitigation |
|------|------------|-----------------|
| Pilot teacher recruitment | Teacher interest | Early engagement, incentives |
| Accessibility compliance | Technical expertise | External accessibility consultant |
| Family consent for pilot | Community trust | Multilingual communication, community liaisons |
| Technology infrastructure | IT resources | Cloud-based solutions, vendor partnerships |

## Decision Gates

| Gate | Timing | Criteria for Proceeding |
|------|--------|------------------------|
| Pilot Launch | Month 5 | Materials pass accessibility and equity review |
| Scaled Implementation | Month 8 | Pilot shows positive outcomes, no major concerns |
| Final Report | Month 12 | Data collection complete, outcomes analyzed |

:::tip Adaptive Planning
While this timeline provides structure, we employ agile principles allowing adjustment based on pilot findings and emerging needs. Quarterly reviews assess progress and authorize plan modifications.
:::
