# Budget

## Funding Request: $50,000

The requested funds will support curriculum development, accessibility implementation, and pilot testing of the Equity-Centered K-12 AI Curriculum over 12 months.

## Budget Allocation

| Category | Amount | Percentage |
|----------|--------|------------|
| Curriculum Development | $20,000 | 40% |
| Accessibility & Inclusion | $12,000 | 24% |
| Professional Development | $8,000 | 16% |
| Assessment & Evaluation | $6,000 | 12% |
| Project Management | $4,000 | 8% |
| **Total** | **$50,000** | **100%** |

## Cost Efficiency

The project leverages significant in-kind contributions, effectively doubling the impact of Stanford's investment:

- **Total project value:** $102,000
- **Stanford investment:** $50,000 (49%)
- **Partner contribution:** $52,000 (51%)

This cost-sharing demonstrates partner commitment and ensures sustainability beyond the grant period.

---

## Sustainability Plan

Beyond the grant period, curriculum continuation is supported by:

1. **District Integration:** Curriculum embedded in standard course offerings
2. **Open-Source Release:** Materials freely available for adaptation
3. **Train-the-Trainer Model:** Capacity built for ongoing PD
4. **Partner Commitment:** El Segundo USD committed to continued implementation

:::info Investment Efficiency
Every dollar of Stanford funding is matched by partner contributions, maximizing impact while building sustainable capacity.
:::
