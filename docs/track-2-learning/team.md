# Project Team

## Leadership

### Charles Sims - Project Director

**Role:** Strategic leadership, curriculum design oversight, community partnerships

Charles Sims is a technology transformation leader at Skafld Studio, where he develops innovative approaches to educational technology implementation. He created the "Hurricane Methodology," a rapid prototyping framework for educational content that emphasizes iterative testing with diverse learners.

**Qualifications:**
- Education background from New York University
- Expertise in technology-enabled learning design
- Track record of equity-focused educational initiatives
- Experience managing complex multi-stakeholder projects

**Project Responsibilities:**
- Overall project direction and accountability
- Curriculum framework design and quality assurance
- Community and employer partnership development
- Equity framework implementation oversight

## Advisory Structure

### Family Advisory Committee

Representatives from El Segundo USD families ensuring community voice in all project decisions:
- Parents of students with disabilities
- Parents of English language learners
- Parents representing socioeconomic diversity
- Student representatives (grades 9-12)

### Educator Advisory Group

District teachers and administrators providing pedagogical expertise:
- Classroom teachers from each grade band
- Special education specialists
- English learner specialists
- Curriculum coordinators

### Technical Advisory Panel

External experts ensuring quality and accessibility:
- Accessibility consultant (WCAG expertise)
- Learning sciences researcher
- AI education specialist
- Assessment design expert

El Segundo USD serves as the implementation partner, providing:
- Student population for curriculum implementation

- Commitment to sustaining curriculum beyond grant period

- Dedicated staff time for project coordination
